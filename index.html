02

The data is the moat

The one thing worth building first.

What ORVÉA sells is knowledge: who the client is, how each home runs, what the family prefers. That accumulated record is the real value, and it is what makes ORVÉA hard to copy.

A competitor can copy an app in a month. They cannot copy years of knowledge about specific families and homes. Off-the-shelf platforms make this worse: they scatter the data across a CRM, a task tool and notes, and never hold it as one structured record.

Done properly, that record is portable to any future system and can sit under any client experience we ever build. Done poorly, it is slow and expensive to fix later.

This is why the technology focus is the data first, before apps or portals.

07

Security

Discretion is the product, so security gets a straight answer.

Now

Secure enough to prove the model

  • Client login
  • Each client sees only their own information
  • Encrypted storage

Low effort on the early tooling, right for a pilot with trusted early clients.

Later · Stage 2

Bank-grade for the most security-conscious client

  • Full data residency control
  • Masking which home is empty
  • Complete audit trails
  • Isolated environments

Not fully solvable on early tooling, under any approach, and so part of Stage 2.

A Stage 2 conversation, by design.

The pilot is built to prove the model with people who trust us, not to onboard the most security-conscious client on day one.
